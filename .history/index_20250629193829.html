AI is for everyone.

Empower your school and your students to thrive in a world of AI!

Day of AI + Common Sense Media Have Teamed Up for Families!

Day of AI isn't just one day

—it's your day to explore AI whenever it fits your schedule! Whether a single lesson or a full event, Day of AI can be run at your school over a day, week, month, or year. Feel free to contact us to help you decide how to bring AI literacy to your students!

We work with schools and school districts to:

Create AI literacy programs for all school stakeholders, including students, teachers, administrators, and families.

Train and educate teachers on how to use AI and AI-enabled tools to support their work.

Ensure student safety by developing responsible and equitable AI policies in their school.

Curriculum

We provide an extensive set of hands-on freely available AI literacy curriculum for K12 students of all backgrounds and abilities.

Professional Development

We offer a number of professional development opportunities for teachers and schools interested in learning more about using AI and basic AI literacy for their various constituents.

Student Safety and AI Policy

We work with schools and school districts in providing guidance on developing appropriate AI policies customized for specific school needs and families.
