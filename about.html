Partners

The task of preparing all schools, teachers, students, and families for the future world of AI is daunting. No organization will succeed independently, which is why we are so grateful for the work and support of our partners and collaborators. At the top of this list is the team at MIT Raise, which started this transformative journey and continues to provide leadership in this important work. In addition, we are fortunate to have a generous group of organizations worldwide who have supported our mission from the beginning.

If you share our mission and are interested in becoming a future partner or collaborator by providing AI educational resources, human capital, and/or financial support to expand our work, please contact us directly.
